Our Story

A women-owned boutique built on elegance, care, and community.

Parul’s Fashion is a small, women-owned boutique in Ashburn, Virginia, where modern silhouettes meet traditional craft. Every piece is handpicked and styled with intention — so you walk out feeling confident, seen, and beautifully dressed for whatever the day asks of you.

We are proudly LGBTQ+ friendly, a transgender safe space, and a welcoming home for anyone who loves great clothes and personal styling done right.
